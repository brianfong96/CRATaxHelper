How to use

  1. Open the T1 General form and enter your income and deduction amounts.
  2. Click "Open BC428 →" — your taxable income (line 26000) transfers automatically.
  3. Complete the BC428 credits and click "← Save & Return to T1".
  4. The T1 updates with your BC provincial tax and shows your refund or balance owing.
  5. Click "📄 Export PDF" on any form to download a filled summary PDF.
  6. Click the ⓘ formula button on any calculated line to see how it is computed.
  7. Use "💾 Save Snapshot" to save a scenario, then visit Compare to see differences.
