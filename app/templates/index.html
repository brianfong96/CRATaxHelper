How to use

  1. Open the T1 General form and enter your income and deduction amounts.
  2. Click "Open BC428" on the T1 form — your taxable income (line 26000) transfers automatically.
  3. Complete the BC428 provincial credits and click "Save & return to T1".
  4. The T1 summary updates with your provincial tax and shows your refund or balance owing.
  5. Use "Save Snapshot" on either form to save a scenario, then visit Compare to see side-by-side differences.
